Documentation of the game

Move in the galaxy

Use the click mouse. Dont forget you've got a mini map at the top right of the galaxie screen !

Move on a planet

Use the keyboard arrow (Left, Right, Down, Up).

Land on a planet

Head to the planet and click on the button "Land" to land your ship on a planet.

Open your inventory

Press I on your keyboard.

Open the quest board

Press L on your keyboard.
